Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket. Known for his calm personality, leadership skills, and powerful finishing, he has inspired millions of cricket fans across the world.

Under his captaincy, India won the ICC T20 World Cup (2007), ICC Cricket World Cup (2011), and ICC Champions Trophy (2013). Dhoni is admired for his wicket-keeping excellence and match-winning abilities.
